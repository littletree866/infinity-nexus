🔒 Privacy & Security

Privacy Policy

v2.0 - April 2026

⚡ Key Privacy Statement

Infinity Nexus is a personal project created by an independent developer. This platform respects user privacy and maintains strict data minimization practices. No personal information is collected, sold, or shared with third parties. User data security and privacy are paramount.

📋 1. Overview & Scope

Infinity Nexus is a personal entertainment platform maintained by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy outlines how user data is (and is not) collected, processed, and protected. All visitors should review this policy before using the platform.

👤 2. Personal Information Collection

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or home address through standard website interaction. Infinity Nexus operates on a strict no-collection policy for personally identifiable information.

⚙️ 3. Automatic Data & Analytics

No automatic data collection or tracking is implemented on this platform. We do not use analytics tracking services, pixel tags, or similar technologies to monitor user behavior. Your browsing activity on Infinity Nexus is not tracked or recorded.

🍪 4. Cookies & Local Storage

Infinity Nexus may use local browser storage only for essential functionality:

• Theme Preference: Saves your light/dark mode selection
• Game Progress: Stores game state data locally (on your device only)
• Session Data: Temporary data required for game functionality

These are first-party cookies only and are never used for cross-site tracking or advertising purposes.

🔗 5. Third-Party Services & Integrations

Infinity Nexus minimizes third-party dependencies. The platform uses:

• Google Fonts: For typography (no tracking)
• Font Awesome: For icon assets (no tracking)
• GitHub: For code hosting and issue management

These services are open-source or transparent. No data is sold to third parties.

📊 6. Data Retention Policy

All locally stored game data is retained on your device until you manually clear your browser data. No server-side database stores user information. Data is not backed up externally.

📢 7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in platform practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on the News & Updates page with version numbers and dates.

💬 8. Contact & Support

For privacy-related questions, data concerns, or inquiries:

• Feedback Form: Submit here
• GitHub Issues: Report concerns via the repository
• GitHub Discussions: Ask questions in the community forum

🎯 9. GDPR & Legal Compliance

While Infinity Nexus does not collect personal data, this platform respects privacy regulations including GDPR. No personal data means no data subject rights requests are necessary. Users retain complete control over their local device data.

📜 10. Summary & Commitment

Your privacy is protected by design. Infinity Nexus operates with transparency and minimal data collection. No tracking, no analytics, no advertising, and no data commercialization. Just games, community, and good vibes. 🎮✨
