Route 101

Route 101 is a short route connecting Littleroot Town to the south and Oldale Town to the north. This is the first route in the game and serves as the player's introduction to wild Pokémon battles.

Upon first entering the route, the player will hear a cry for help from Professor Birch, who is being chased by a wild Pokémon. After selecting a starter Pokémon from his bag and defeating the attacker, the player officially begins their journey.

Afterward, Route 101 becomes a simple early-game training area with low-level Pokémon, making it ideal for gaining initial experience.

▶ Tips

  • This is the first place to train your starter Pokémon safely.
  • Poochyena is the most common encounter and can be useful early on.
  • Zigzagoon can have the Ability Pickup, making it valuable for collecting items.
  • Return here later if you need quick, low-risk experience.
